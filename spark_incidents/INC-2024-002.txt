Incident ID: INC-2024-002
Date: 2024-03-01
Cluster: Spark-Analytics-Cluster-B
Application: Ad Click ETL Job
Error Summary: FetchFailedException: Shuffle block cannot be found on disk.
Root Cause: Insufficient disk space on shuffle node; shuffle spill data was removed prematurely.
Resolution:
  - Added additional shuffle disk volume.
  - Configured spark.local.dir to use multiple mount points.
  - Enabled external shuffle service for resiliency.
Key Learnings: Monitor disk I/O and shuffle spill directories in large shuffles.
